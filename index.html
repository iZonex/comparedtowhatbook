Why this book exists

The book was not planned. For years its author documented crypto incidents in a public research catalogue — classifying every major theft, fraud and collapse since 2010, with a citation for every claim. Somewhere past the six hundredth incident, a pattern became impossible to ignore: most of these crimes were not new. The technology changed. The financial mechanisms didn't.
